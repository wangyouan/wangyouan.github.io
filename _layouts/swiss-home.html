Youan Wang

Associate Professor of Finance

WISE & School of Economics, Xiamen University

I am an Associate Professor of Finance at the Wang Yanan Institute for Studies in Economics (WISE) and the School of Economics, Xiamen University. I received my Ph.D. in Finance from the University of Hong Kong. My research examines how political institutions, regulation, and stakeholder relations shape corporate decisions and financial markets, with particular interests in empirical corporate finance, political economy, corporate social responsibility, and environmental finance. I am also interested in applying machine learning and artificial intelligence to financial research. My work has been published in Management Science, Review of Finance, Journal of Corporate Finance, and Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis.
